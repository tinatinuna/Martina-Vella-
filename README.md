  <h1>MALTESE DOORS</h1>

  <p>For my first assignment for DGA1020, Applied Software Practices, I had to design a vector illustration of a Maltese door.</p>
  <br>
  <h2>Visual Research</h2>

  <p> In order to start designing the door, I first had to research the different doors that exist on the Maltese Islands.</p>
  <p> To do so, I captured at least 25 photographs of 25 different Maltese doors.</p>
